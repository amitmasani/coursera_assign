OUR SPECIAL MENU

MOMOs

Momo is a type of East and South Asian steamed filled dumpling, popular across the Indian subcontinent and the Himalayan regions of South Asia

Samosa

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savoury filling, such as spiced potatoes, onions, peas, cheese, beef and other meats, or lentils.

Fries

Fries, chips, finger chips, or French-fried potatoes, are batonnet or allumette-cut deep-fried potatoes.
